ACL Rehabilitation Analysis: Investigating the Relationship Between AMI Scores and TSK-11 Outcomes

Overview: 
At Davis Physical Therapy and Sports Rehab, patients took Tampa Scale-11 (TSK-11) exams to see how they personally viewed their conditions. Additionally, patients did Arthrogenic Muscle Inhibtion (AMI) tests throughout their rehibilitation process.

Patients from these data collections had various injuries such as ankles, backs, shoulders and hips. But this project specifically analyzes the relationship between AMI scores and TSK-11 scores in the case of ACL injuries.
